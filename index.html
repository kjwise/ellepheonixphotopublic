About

Originally from the mountainous country of Georgia and now based in Iceland, Elle Phoenix photographs the quiet relationship between people and place. With a minimalist approach, her work explores light, stillness, and scale.

Available for portrait sessions, editorial commissions, and 1:1 or small-group photo tours around Iceland.
